Crepes

How to do them

This is a simple but delicious reciple for crepes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 500 ml of milk
  • 250g of flour
  • 125g of sugar
  • 4 table spoons of oil
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients in a mixing bowl
  2. Mix everything with an electric whisk until you get an homegenous misture
